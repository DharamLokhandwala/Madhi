Happily serving customers for 78+ years

Madhi Ni Khamni was established at Madhi village which is located in Gujarat, India. More than 78 years ago, Shri Tribhovandas Ghelabhai found Madhi Ni Khamni through his beliefs in the well-being of the society and providing the best nutrition possible. He created the best ever and most popular breakfast khamni which is being loved by people till today. Currently, we manufacture our products in surat which is a well-known city in India. Our warehouse is regularly cleaned and sanitized because when it comes to our food, we do care about our customers.

We are driven and inspired by our purpose, and that is "Food that matters for life. We provide varieties like Khaman, Khamni, Samosa, Petis, Patra, Patudi, Idla, sev, Mix Farshan, and much more. We retail as well as we accept big orders for weddings, parties, birthdays, anniversaries and lot more. We also support our local community by giving away leftover food to the needy people for free. We make everything fresh and from scratch and nothing is served stale. As a part of this community, we promise to provide with the best food and services because we have a lot more to offer for generations to come in future
