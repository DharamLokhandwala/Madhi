Who are we?

Madhi Ni Khamni was established at Madhi village which is located in Gujarat, India. More than 78 years ago, Shri Tribhovandas Ghelabhai found Madhi Ni Khamni through his beliefs in the well-being of the society and providing the best nutrition possible. He created the best ever and most popular breakfast khamni which is being loved by people till today. Currently, we manufacture our products in surat which is a well-known city in India.
